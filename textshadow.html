with text-shadow.
1) text-shadow:2px 2px 3px red;(h-shadow v-shadow blur-radius color)

2) The value of h-shadow and y-shadow can be negative as shown below.
text-shadow:-2px -2px 3px red;

3) We can also give multiple shadow as shown below.
text-shadow:4px 4px 1px red,8px 8px 1px green,12px 12px 1px blue;
