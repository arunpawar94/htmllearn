The different way of writting padding code are:
		1.padding-top:10px;       (space from top)
		  padding-right:20px;     (space from right)
		  padding-bottom:30px;    (space from bottom)
		  padding-left:40px;      (space from left)
		
		2.padding:10px 20px 30px 40px; (space from top right bottom left resp.)
		
		3.padding:10px   (space of 10px from top right bottom and left)
		
		4.padding:10px 20px; (space of 10px from top and bottom & 20px from left and right)
